Shuochen Su

I am a Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs, working on computer vision algorithms and computational imaging systems for machine perception in AR/VR. My research interests span the fields of computer vision, computer graphics, machine learning, computational photography, and computational imaging.

Before Meta, I was a research assistant in Wolfgang Heidrich's lab at UBC, where I received a Ph.D. in computer science in 2018. I obtained my bachelor's degree in computer science from Tsinghua University in 2013. My past affiliations as an intern / visiting student include MSR Asia, KAUST, Adobe Research, and Stanford University.
